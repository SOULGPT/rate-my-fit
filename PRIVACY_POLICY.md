# Privacy Policy for Rate My Fit

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2025

## Introduction
Rate My Fit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Photos**: Outfit photos you upload for AI analysis
- **Stickers**: Custom stickers you create and choose to share

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App interaction data, features used
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type
- **Analytics**: Basic analytics to improve app performance

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Analyze your outfit photos using AI (Google Gemini)
- Generate ratings and recommendations
- Create and share stickers (if you choose to share)
- Improve our services and user experience
- Maintain app security and prevent fraud

## Data Storage and Security

- **Images**: Processed in real-time but not stored on our servers
- **Stickers**: Stored in Firebase Realtime Database if you choose to share publicly
- **Security**: We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- **Encryption**: Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Gemini AI**: For outfit analysis (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
- **Firebase**: For database and hosting (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect additional data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your shared stickers
- Opt-out of data collection (by not using certain features)
- Request information about data we've collected

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your photos with third parties (except AI processing)
- Use your photos for any purpose other than providing our service

If you share stickers publicly, they will be visible to other app users.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
